Cow Bull

A word guessing game

Game Rules:

  • The player chooses the word length, either 4, 5 or 6 letters.
  • The player chooses the difficulty level, Easy (20 guesses), Medium (15 guesses) or Hard (10 guesses).
  • The game generates a random word of the chosen length.
  • The player has to guess the word within the given number of attempts.
  • After each guess, the game will provide feedback in the form of 'cows' and 'bulls'.
  • A 'cow' means a letter is in the correct position, and a 'bull' means a letter is in the word but in the wrong position.
